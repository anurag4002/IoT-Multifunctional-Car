Bluetooth Controlling

In this Car we have embeded Blutooth Module by which we can control the car using Bluetooth. We are implmenting Bluetooth version 5.0 which is the latest version of Bluetooth. Here we use Bluetooth module that has a limitation of range. Will permit the client to control the vehicle from anyplace in the required location. Will permit the vehicle to be prepared, control or to advance without anyone else so it turns out to be genuinely independent and can work without human intercession of any sorts.

Voice Controlling

We have implemented voice assistant to control the Car using voice command. So, we are forwarding into new era with Powerfull Technologies in modern way. The voice controlling feature in my project, the "voice controlling car," allows you to control the car's movements using your voice. Instead of using buttons or a remote control, you can simply give commands like "forward," "backward," "turn left," or "turn right" and the car will respond accordingly. This feature adds convenience and a hands-free experience, making it easier to operate the car and have fun with it.

Obstacles Detecting

An Arduino obstacle detector is a device that helps detect obstacles in a simple and easy way. It uses sensors to identify objects in its path and sends signals to the Arduino board. These signals are then processed to trigger appropriate actions, such as stopping a robot or activating an alarm. This technology is widely used in robotics and automation to prevent collisions and improve safety.

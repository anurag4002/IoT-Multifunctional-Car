L298 Motor Driver

The L298 Motor Driver is a component used to control motors in simple electronic circuits. It allows us to control the speed and direction of DC motors. It can also be used with stepper motors. The L298 Motor Driver is popular because it provides an easy way to connect and control motors, making it useful in various projects such as robotics and automation.

Bluetooth Module

HC-05 is a Bluetooth module which is designed for wireless communication. A Bluetooth Module is a small electronic device that enables wireless communication between devices over short distances. It uses Bluetooth technology to establish connections and exchange data wirelessly. Bluetooth Modules are commonly used in various applications such as wireless audio systems, smart home devices, and wearable technology. They provide a convenient and efficient way to connect and communicate between devices without the need for physical cables.

Servo Motor

A Servo Motor is a small device that helps control the movement of objects. It consists of a motor and a feedback mechanism that ensures precise positioning. Servo Motors are commonly used in robotics, remote-controlled vehicles, and other applications where accurate and controlled movement is required. They can rotate to specific angles and hold that position, making them useful for tasks that demand precision and stability.

Gear Motor

A Gear Motor is a type of motor that incorporates gears to provide increased torque and control over rotational speed. It combines the functions of a motor and a gearbox in one compact unit. Gear Motors are widely used in various applications, such as robotics, automation, and machinery, where high torque and precise speed control are necessary. They are efficient, reliable, and help transmit power effectively while reducing the rotational speed.

Robot Wheel

A Robot Wheel is a round component designed to facilitate movement for robots. It is typically made of a durable material such as rubber or plastic and features a tread or pattern on its surface to enhance traction. Robot wheels come in various sizes and designs to suit different robot configurations and environments. These wheels enable robots to navigate and traverse different surfaces, allowing for efficient and controlled movement in robotics applications.

Foam Board

Foam Board is a lightweight and durable material used for various purposes. It is made of a foam core sandwiched between two layers of paper or plastic. Foam boards are easy to work with, as they can be cut, shaped, and decorated. They are commonly used in crafts, presentations, and school projects. Foam boards provide a sturdy surface for mounting artwork or displaying information, making them a versatile choice in many creative and educational settings.

Li-Ion Battery

A Li-ion (Lithium-ion) battery is a rechargeable energy storage device commonly used in electronic devices like smartphones, laptops, and electric vehicles. It provides a high energy density, allowing for longer battery life. Li-ion batteries are lightweight, making them portable and convenient. They are known for their stability and ability to hold a charge over multiple cycles. Li-ion batteries have revolutionized portable electronics by providing a reliable and efficient power source.

Arduino UNO

Arduino UNO is a popular microcontroller board used for creating interactive electronic projects. It features a simple design with various input and output pins that allow users to connect sensors, actuators, and other components easily. Arduino UNO is beginner-friendly, as it can be programmed using a user-friendly software interface. It enables people to explore and experiment with electronics and coding, making it a valuable tool for learning and prototyping projects.

IR Sensor

An IR (Infrared) sensor is a device that detects infrared radiation emitted by objects. It consists of an emitter and a receiver that work together to detect changes in infrared light patterns. IR sensors are commonly used in applications like proximity sensing, obstacle detection, and line-following robots. They provide a reliable and non-contact method for detecting objects or measuring distances, making them useful in various automation and robotics projects.

Ultrasonic Sensor

An Ultrasonic sensor is a device that uses sound waves to measure distances and detect objects. It emits high-frequency sound waves and measures the time it takes for the waves to bounce back after hitting an object. This data is used to calculate the distance to the object. Ultrasonic sensors are commonly used in robotics, security systems, and automation. They provide a non-contact and accurate method for object detection and distance measurement.

Jumper Wire

Jumper wires are essential components used in electronics projects for connecting various electronic elements together. They are flexible wires with pre-stripped ends that can be easily plugged into breadboards, connectors, or other components. Jumper wires eliminate the need for soldering, allowing for quick and temporary connections. They provide a convenient and versatile solution for establishing electrical connections between different components, making prototyping and experimentation more accessible for beginners and professionals alike.

IR Receiver Module

An IR receiver module is a device that receives and detects infrared signals from an IR transmitter. It is commonly used in remote control systems, allowing devices to receive commands wirelessly. The IR receiver module captures the transmitted infrared signals and converts them into electrical signals that can be interpreted by a microcontroller or other electronic components. It enables devices to communicate and respond to commands sent via infrared technology.

On / Off Switch

An on-off switch is a simple mechanical device used to control the flow of electricity in a circuit. It allows users to turn a device or circuit on or off by either closing or opening the circuit. When the switch is in the "on" position, it completes the electrical path, allowing the current to flow. Conversely, when the switch is in the "off" position, it interrupts the electrical path, preventing the current from flowing, effectively shutting off the device or circuit. On-off switches are commonly found in everyday devices like lamps, appliances, and electronic gadgets.
